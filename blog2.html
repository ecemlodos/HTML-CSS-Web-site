ANIMALS

Animals are our companions, our workers, our eyes and ears, and our food. They appear in ancient cave paintings, and on modern commercial farms. We have domesticated some of them, while others remain wild and are sometimes endangered by our activities. They keep us company, and while they can provide comic relief, they also serve us as valuable assistants.

DOGS
  • Feeding a dog
    1. Feed your dog a high quality dog food and on a regular schedule.
    2. Avoid giving your dog an excessive amount of treats or people food.
    3. Give your dog water.
  • Caring for the health of a dog
    1. Get your dog vaccinated.
    2. Consider fitting the dog with an ID chip.
    3. Use regular parasite preventative treatments.
    4. Consider desexing your dog.
  • Groom your dog regularly.
  • Exercising, Socializing, and Playing With Your Dog
    1. Provide your dog with plenty of exercise.
    2. Choose good toys for your dog
    3. Stimulate your dog's mind with reward-based training.
    4. Socialize your dog.
    5. Allow the dog to play and display behaviors that are normal for the breed.

CATS
  1. Groom your cat regularly.
  2. Provide fresh water daily.
  3. Make sure you have enough litter boxes.
  4. Notice if your cat starts urinating outside its litter box.
  5. Train your cat to use a scratching post.
  6. Keep your cat's teeth clean.
  7. Schedule regular veterinary visits.
  8. Spay or neuter your cat.
